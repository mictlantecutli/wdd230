Target Audience

The SE chamber grouping native entrepreneurs of all the Hidalgo state, it was created in 2022, with the interest of different merchant groups, artisans, field producers, indigenous fabric groups, indigenous artists. So the target is, native people that have the interest to keep indigenous values and apply it to the business and commercial activities, in this way develop the economic activity with identity. Usually the audience of this web page is native between 25-40 years old, in general are young people that decide to stay in their towns working in familiar commercial activities and look to grow their enterprises.So this web chamber help to they to connect with the economy of all the Hidalgo state and the country, and in this way help others to not forget the cultural roots.

Personas

Juana Gutiérrez Hernández

She works in weaving and textiles. She is 25 years old, she is the eldest daughter of her family, she has 3 brothers and 2 daughters. Her father went to The United States 20 years ago. Her office is a weaver of textiles, she learned from her mother. She found in the teaching of hepr mother one way to help her family, because in her house they live with many families, so she decided with her mother and sisters to start a cloth business of cloth by weaving. She has the goal to export her clothes to another country. She has for the moment one small store in a touristic place in Hidalgo State. In the afternoon she spend the time to rest in family, she see tv programs, she talk with her family about the day, talk about the family and how grow the familiar business, she have interest in upload the cloth business in internet.

Octaviano Mendoza

He is a craftsman, He makes masks, He has a masks workshop, He is 51 years old. He is married, He has four sons and one dougthe. He is a grandfather. He had exhibited his art work in important places in Mexico City. He wants to mount a web page about his decorative masks. He has many clients in Mexico, because of the ancient traditions, there are a lot of festivities in which the people use different masks. He likes to hear music, He work the mud also, He all the time is working with his hands. He speaks Nahuatl language, his parents teach him, He tries to teach his family his language while He practices it. He always remembers their parents, she talks with his family about the ancients, about his teachings and customs, he likes the tradition of death day.
