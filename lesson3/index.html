Purpose

Hidalgo state is in the center of the Mexican Republic. In this state there are a lot of people with native language, such as Nahuatl, Otomi, Tepehua, in total, according to one dependence of the government there are 360,000 speakers of native language in Hidalgo state. But through the time they had been marginalized, so the purpose of this chamber, integrate them into the economy of the region and not forget the native languages. Because of that the name of the chamber has a name in ancient language; SE is the number one, but that word has its origin in the seed, because the seed is the first or the start of all the things.
